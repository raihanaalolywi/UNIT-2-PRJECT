AI IN SAUDI ARABIA

Saudi Arabia is rapidly embracing artificial intelligence (AI) to drive innovation and economic growth. The government has launched initiatives like the National Strategy for Data and AI, aiming to position the country as a global leader in AI by 2030. Key sectors benefiting from AI include healthcare, smart cities, finance, and education, with significant investments in research and development to foster a thriving AI ecosystem.

AI in Healthcare

**Artificial intelligence in medical devices across Saudi hospitals** is rapidly advancing, transforming healthcare quality and efficiency. AI is used for **medical imaging analysis, early disease detection, and remote patient monitoring**. Smart systems also assist in **managing electronic health records and predicting emergencies** before they occur. The **Ministry of Health and SDAIA** are expanding AI adoption to enhance accuracy and reduce medical errors. Saudi Arabia is becoming a **leader in integrating AI technologies into its healthcare system** for safer and smarter medical care.
